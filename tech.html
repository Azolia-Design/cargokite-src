Airborne Wind Energy System (AWE)

The "Kite" soars at an impressive altitude of 300 meters, reaching the sweet spot for harvesting high-altitude winds, which are available in nearly 95% of locations worldwide.

This is the result of collaborative efforts, developed by visionary partners who've harnessed the power of these winds for a more sustainable future.

Hydrofoils

Our innovative underwater wings, positioned beneath the ship's hull, elevate the vessel, minimizing water contact and reducing drag.

These wings are not only actively controllable but also adept at counteracting the forces generated by the (AWE) system. Developed in-house, they represent a pivotal element of our cutting-edge technology.

Ship hull

CargoKite's approach extends beyond design; our vessel is co-designed by CargoKite and manufactured by our trusted partner shipyard.

The optimized hull facilitates efficient foil-borne take-off and smooth transitions. It also serves as a crucial interface for transmitting forces from the AWES to the hydrofoils, vital for structural integrity.

Route Planning Tool

Our in-house-developed system calculates the optimal routes for CargoKite ships based on real-time weather conditions, ensuring efficient and safe navigation.

Additionally, it excels in providing highly accurate Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA) predictions, allowing for precise planning and scheduling.

Control & Intelligence Software

At the heart of our innovation lies CargoKite's core Intellectual Property (IP), a game-changer that seamlessly synchronizes the operation of the kite and hydrofoils.

This groundbreaking technology not only ensures their smooth coordination but also maintains full situational awareness for automatic and efficient operation.
